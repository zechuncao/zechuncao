Overview

Modify the GeometricObject class in Lab9.java to implement the Comparable interface, and define a static max method in the GeometricObject class for finding the larger of two GeometricObject objects. Implement the new GeometricObject class, write a test program that uses the max method to find the larger of two circles and the larger of two rectangles.
